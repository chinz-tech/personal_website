Welcome to My Website

Hello, my name is Zachary Chin! This website is an introduction to who I am and what I care about. I am especially passionate about consulting/finance, data, leadership, and using analytical thinking to solve real-world problems. A big part of what motivates me is the idea that numbers are not just numbers—they tell stories about businesses, people, markets, and decisions. That is one reason I enjoy studying finance and data science: finance gives me a lens for understanding how companies grow, compete, and create value, while data science helps me explore patterns, test ideas, and communicate insights in a clear way.

Beyond academics, I also enjoy leadership and collaboration. On-campus, I am the President of GRC, an impact consulting group. I find it incredibly rewarding to contribute to projects that have a meaningful outcome, whether that means creating a presentation, conducting analysis, or helping guide a group toward a shared goal. I also love watching sports, cooking and baking, and trying new restaurants!

Another reason I wanted to create this site is that it reflects a skill set that is becoming more and more important: building and presenting your work online. A personal website is a simple but powerful way to showcase your interests, personality, and experiences. It also gives me a chance to learn more about HTML, CSS, and web publishing through GitHub Pages. Overall, this website highlights both who I am and the areas I am excited to keep exploring in the future.

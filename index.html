Welcome to My Website

Hi, my name is Zachary Chin, and this website is a short introduction to who I am and what I care about. I am especially passionate about finance, data, leadership, and using analytical thinking to solve real-world problems. A big part of what motivates me is the idea that numbers are not just numbers—they tell stories about businesses, people, markets, and decisions. That is one reason I enjoy studying finance and data science. Finance gives me a lens for understanding how companies grow, compete, and create value, while data science helps me explore patterns, test ideas, and communicate insights in a clear way.

Beyond academics, I also enjoy leadership and collaboration. I value working with teams, building organizations, and helping people develop their ideas. I find it rewarding to contribute to projects that have a meaningful outcome, whether that means creating a presentation, conducting analysis, or helping guide a group toward a shared goal. I am especially interested in roles and opportunities where business strategy and problem-solving come together.

Another reason I wanted to create this site is because it reflects a skill set that is becoming more and more important: building and presenting your work online. A personal website is a simple but powerful way to showcase your interests, personality, and experiences. It also gives me a chance to learn more about HTML, CSS, and web publishing through GitHub Pages. Overall, this website highlights both who I am and the areas I am excited to keep exploring in the future.
